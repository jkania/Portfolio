About

Who Am I?

I am a systems professional with over 15 years of experience in the SDLC, always focused on the User Experience. I've held roles in Design, Development, Research, and Analysis.

I started my Development career on the mainframe, coding with Cobol and JCL. I then moved on to work on applications for both Client Server and Web.

I have a Bachelor of Science (June 2001) and a Master of Science (June 2015) in Human-Computer Interaction, and I am currently working on a Certificate program at DePaul University in Front End Web Development (to be completed 11/2015).

I drive UI requirements from concept to release. I define features, user flows, content and error conditions. I am also able to do most of the coding to bring the product to life.

On a personal note, I'm a wife and a mom to 2 boys (ages 10 and 13) and 1 furbaby (Buddy the dog). I was born and raised in Chicago and moved to Carmel, Indiana for a job opportunity for my husband in August 2015.
